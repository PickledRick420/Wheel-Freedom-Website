About

At Wheel Freedom, we’re revolutionizing outdoor adventures with our thrilling eco-friendly electric unicycles! Experience the ultimate rush as you zoom around while competing in action-packed laser tag, Nerf/airsoft/paintball battles, or splash-tastic water gun tournaments!   Join a local league for a chance to win real money!

Dive into an extraordinary adventure by applying for membership in your local pact! Experience personalized city tours, thrilling group escapades, and the chance to connect with amazing new people. Join us for an unforgettable journey filled with excitement, laughter, and friendly competition that will truly get your adrenaline pumping! Don't miss out on the fun—let's create incredible memories together!

These unforgettable experiences are perfect for birthdays, corporate events, or just a thrilling day of wet summer fun with friends and family! Our affordable hourly rentals make it easy to dive into the excitement, and for those looking for an extra edge, you can also hire a skilled instructor or game referee on demand. Don't miss out on the action—make a splash and create lasting memories!
